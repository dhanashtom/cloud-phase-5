A Week in bangkok

Date: April 20, 2023

The Grand Palace is a magnificent complex that served as the official residence of the Thai Kings for centuries. Within the palace grounds, you'll find Wat Phra Kaew, the Temple of the Emerald Buddha, which is Thailand's most sacred temple.

Our first stop is the world-famous street food scene in Bangkok. Brace yourselves for a culinary adventure as we dive into the aromatic flavors of Pad Thai, Tom Yum Goong, and Mango Sticky Rice. Trust me; your taste buds are in for a treat! Treat yourself to a relaxing Thai massage or spa treatment. Bangkok has numerous spas and wellness centers offering traditional Thai massages, herbal treatments, and rejuvenating therapies. Escape the city's hustle and bustle by visiting Lumpini Park, a green oasis in the heart of Bangkok. You can enjoy leisure activities, boat rides, and watch monitor lizards roaming freely.
